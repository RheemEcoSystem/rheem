Call for dRHEEMers

There is a consensus that the data analytics space is too broad to aim for a “one size fits all” system. A side effect of this consensus is that a specific data analytics task get tightly coupled with a highly specialized system. This makes it harder to adapt and upgrade applications when either a new task comes along or a new system becomes available. This calls for more freedom in data analytics.

If you also dream of freedom in data analytics, then join RHEEM!

We are looking for highly motivated dRHEEMers (postdocs) to help us in realizing our dRHEEM. A postdoc @ QCRI is two years offering with great compensation and benefits. The positions are available immediately and applications will be accepted on a continuing basis until all positions are filled.

Requirements?

  1. Ph.D. in Computer Science (or related)
  2. background in data management
  3. solid coding skills
  4. strong publication record

Want to join our dRHEEM?
Please send us (jquianeruiz@qf.org.qa) your complete CV and your three representative papers.

Revolution place?
The positions are in the Data Analytics centre (DA) at QCRI. DA's research is mainly on data curation, big data infrastructure, and applied machine learning. Please see DA's webpage for more details of DA’s projects.
