A scalable system for big data cleaning

BigDansing is a Big Data Cleansing system that tackles efficiency, scalability, and ease-of-use issues in data cleansing. It leverages Rheem to allow users to easily express their data quality rules, with no requirement of being aware of the underlying data processing platform. Moreover, it exploits the extensibility and adaptability of Rheem to plug a series of physical optimizations for boosting performance. Thanks to this, BigDansing is not only able to offer an easy-to-use rule specification, but also to provide orders of magnitude better performance than baseline systems. [more info]
